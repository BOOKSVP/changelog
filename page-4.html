13 May 2024

Gift Aid Feature & Usability Fixes

New

Collect Gift Aid - UK Accounts

You can now enable Gift Aid collection for those based int he UK collecting Donations. If your account is registered in the UK, simple click 'Collect Gift Aid' in the Settings of the associated Event.

Turning this setting ON will show a Gift Aid collection box on the first page of the booking process. In order to enable this box you MUST also enable Donations in your Event Settings.

When a user checks this box, they will be require to enter their billing address on the checkout page if they have decided to donate.

Fixed

  • Reports indicated that accounts were being assigned a default space with no Events. The default Space you are assigned is now linked the the events you are a member of. If you only have access to a single event, your default space is now the associated Space with that Event.
26 April 2024

Booking Link Upgrades and Improved App Performance

New

Mark Booking Links as 'Register Interest'

You can now configure your links to be Register Interest links. Register Interest links will mark a booking as 'registered' in your list of bookings. You then have control to Accept or Reject bookings manually.

⚠️ As a result, we have sunset 'register interest' Invites

New

Create unique Booking Links for Portals

You now have the ability to create a unique Booking Link for each of your Exhibitor Portals. The Exhibitor will then see the link in their Portal and any subsequent bookings made via the link will show up in the bookings tab for the Exhibitor.

⚠️ If you assign multiple Portals to a single Booking Link, all portals will gain access to any bookings made via this link.

Improved

Much faster booking list load time

We have significantly improved the performance of our Booking List page load times. In some instances we are seeing a 500% increase in speed.

Improved

Personalised messaging

Our personalise feature to add custom content in your messaging such as {{name}} and {{email}} has now been improved to support {{first_name}} and {{last_name}}

Fixed

  • Filtering your Bookings via Tags now takes your search and date queries into account

12th April 2024

New

New Pricing Structure

To make ARTSVP even easier to use we've modified our pricing structure to a simpler usage-based model. This means no more dealing with invoices back and forth or waiting for contracts to be signed before you can get off the ground and start managing your events. With this new change, all accounts will now have access to the full suite of ARTSVP features, but will pay for our VIP features of invites and portals on an as you need basis.

All the information you'll need to self manage your account will be on your billing dashboard as seen above. From here you'll be able to quickly purchase the invites or portals you need for your event through Stripe. All new and existing accounts (with exceptions) will automatically be given 100 free invites to continue using the platform, and 3 portals to test the feature, if you need more simply click the top right icon to purchase more.

Better Financial Insights

To provide better insight into ticket sales and revenues, two new charts have been added in the analytics view for a space. These two charts provide insights on buying patterns and trends to help you make better decisions related to marketing campaigns, mailers, etc. See for yourself here.

25th March 2024

New

Take Payments at the Door!

We're beta launching our latest product feature, Stripe POS terminal integrations. What does this mean for you? Bygone are the days of having customers queue up at the door buying tickets through the ARTSVP site. Instead, your front of house staff will be able to take bookings right there on the spot and process payments immediately through the use of Stripe BBPOS WisePOS E terminals.

Integration is as simple as requesting you'd like to take part in our new feature here. Once we confirm your request, you'll order as many terminals as you need for your event and register the device(s) through our site.

New

Promo Codes Launched

We're excited to announce the launch of our new percentage-based promo codes! This feature allows users to apply a single discount code you've set to take a percentage off the final booking price (excludes donations, one promotional code per booking allowed).

Whether it's a special promotion or a reward for VIPs, you can configure multiple promo codes per event with varying percentages. Manage promo codes and track usage in the Edit section for your event under the Promo Codes tab.
