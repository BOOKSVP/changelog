## New
 

This week we're rolling out Unique Bookings. You can now restrict events to only allow bookings from unique emails. No more double bookings!

- Turn on/off for any event in your event settings
- Only allows one booking per email address
- Works with personalised invites (locked to an email)
- Use in conjunction with Invites and Collections to allow users to book once each for a selection of events.
