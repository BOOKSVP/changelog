## New
 
Take Payments at the Door!

We're beta launching our latest product feature, Stripe POS terminal integrations. What does this mean for you? Bygone are the days of having customers queue up at the door buying tickets through the ARTSVP site. Instead, your front of house staff will be able to take bookings right there on the spot and process payments immediately through the use of Stripe BBPOS WisePOS E terminals.

![Screenshot 2024-03-25 at 17.33.45.png](https://cloud.headwayapp.co/changelogs_images/images/big/000/124/915-176f92b61aa06847a68262c477076c8a067b764e.png)

Integration is as simple as requesting you'd like to take part in our new feature [here](https://forms.reform.app/CWgy53/pos-request/0l8rlv?eb641963-b64d-46ac-a913-60be0911b78b=Sy%20Rashid&2693adc9-26ff-427c-a84a-0483758fbbe6=sy@artsvp.com&d9f1d2af-60cd-4e76-aad1-11e60fd4bd8d=That%20Hot%20Ish).  Once we confirm your request, you'll order as many terminals as you need for your event and register the device(s) through our site.
