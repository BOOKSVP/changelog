## New
 
Feature Updates

This week, we've made significant enhancements to our Exhibitor Portals.

## Program Invites for Exhibitor Portals thru Collections

In the past, Exhibitor Portals were limited to inviting guests solely to Events. However, with our latest feature, exhibitors can now extend invitations to guests for a variety of occasions, including entire VIP Programs. Allocating these invites for fairs has never been easier, thanks to an improved allocation interface for both Collections and Events.

- De-duplication of bookings made using portal invites
- Time Zone selection in All Bookings view
- Dynamic Collection Invite use count, eliminating the need for tedious manual updates
- Inline tag editing for Invites

## Fixed

- Pagination fixes applied across multiple pages
