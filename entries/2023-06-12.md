## New
 

## Feature Updates

Add to Wallet

Guests can now effortlessly store their tickets in their smartphone wallet for easy access and quick entry scanning. See how it works with an example pass for [Apple Wallet](https://artsvp.com/pass?ref=gue-9d6c60cb-7be5-408e-b410-594554ee3624&type=apple) or [Google Wallet](https://artsvp.com/pass?ref=gue-9d6c60cb-7be5-408e-b410-594554ee3624&type=google)

![Red.png](//cloud.headwayapp.co/changelogs_images/images/big/000/109/604-199d234cecc0d55aa9ad4036169d12377b39674b.png)

Banners

All users now have the functionality to enhance their booking pages with banners. Additionally, you can now personalize confirmation and reminder emails by adding a banner image at the top. [Find out more.](https://artsvp.crisp.help/en/article/adding-banners-1bx76b4/?bust=1686567402287)

![102.jpg](//cloud.headwayapp.co/changelogs_images/images/big/000/109/605-f7637d1b393f148da3138e42ee048c5b06e3994d.jpg)

Event Waitlists

Now, visitors have the option to join a waitlist for fully booked events or time slots, providing an effective method to expand your mailing list. Additionally, these waitlist bookings can be swiftly confirmed if capacity increases or cancellations occur. [Find out more.](https://artsvp.crisp.help/en/article/allow-people-to-join-a-waitlist-1otaw69/?bust=1686570313797)

Unique bookings

Prevent repeat bookings for the same event by enforcing a 'one booking per email' policy. This keeps your data clean by reducing the likelihood of multiple reservations for the same event. [Find out more.](https://artsvp.crisp.help/en/article/force-unique-bookings-1e7kihs/?bust=1686573313628)

Accept Donations

Are you a charity? Looking for an easy way to handle donations when clients attend your events? You can now accept donations in addition to selling Tickets on the same page. [Request access.](https://forms.reform.app/CWgy53/request-access-to-accept-donations/p8mg9g)

## Premium Features

Sending invites

You can now send personalized invitations directly from ARTSVP. Inviting VIPs is fast, with the added benefits of high deliverability and easy reminders to book. [Request access.](https://forms.reform.app/CWgy53/request-access-to-send-invites/p8mg9g)

Exhibitor Portals

Enable your exhibitors to send their own invites and monitor their guests' attendance at the fair with a real-time exhibitor portal. This not only resolves GDPR issues but also reduces the need for galleries to request names to be added to the list. [Find out more.](https://artsvp.crisp.help/en/category/exhibitor-portals-1u695ni/)

Technical Updates

The team has worked hard at enhancing our API by structuring JSON outputs across the board, expanding our range of endpoints and providing [brand new documentation.](https://docs.artsvp.com)

You may have also noticed some in-app speed improvements as we make continuous efforts to optimize pages that are event-critical. The Bookings view for each event now supports skeleton loading and a 5x speed increase on loading booking data.
