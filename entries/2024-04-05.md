[new]
 
New Pricing Structure

To make ARTSVP even easier to use we've modified our pricing structure to a simpler usage-based model. This means no more dealing with invoices back and forth or  waiting for contracts to be signed before you can get off the ground and start managing your events. With this new change, all accounts will now have access to the full suite of ARTSVP features, but will pay for our VIP features of invites and portals on an as you need basis.

![Screenshot 2024-04-05 at 12.54.53.png](https://cloud.headwayapp.co/changelogs_images/images/big/000/125/512-b8be5d0902056ee060c7ed0161a371261813ae28.png)

All the information you'll need to self manage your account will be on your [billing dashboard](https://app.artsvp.com/settings/billing) as seen above. From here you'll be able to quickly purchase the invites or portals you need for your event through Stripe. All new and existing accounts (with exceptions) will automatically be given 100 free invites to continue using the platform, and 3 portals to test the feature, if you need more simply click the top right icon to purchase more.

![Screenshot 2024-04-05 at 12.56.19.png](https://cloud.headwayapp.co/changelogs_images/images/big/000/125/513-c15adaa44aa1fd5f3b692be3f651413136899c69.png)

Better Financial Insights

To provide better insight into ticket sales and revenues, two new charts have been added in the analytics view for a space. These two charts provide insights on buying patterns and trends to help you make better decisions related to marketing campaigns, mailers, etc.  See for yourself [here](https://app.artsvp.com/analytics).

[improved]
 

- Better CSV handling for header rows and Portal CSV uploads

- Event cards now display on and off status
- Improved scanner UX for Single Entry CheckIns
- Improved data privacy for Portals
- Error logs provided on Invite downloads
