## New

- Portal WhatsApp Notifications — As we all know, the art world runs on WhatsApp. To seamlessly integrate ARTSVP into your existing workflow, we're launching WhatsApp Notifications starting with Portals, ensuring you stay effortlessly up to date. Your exhibitors can receive real-time WhatsApp alerts when their VIPs walk through the door.

- Accept Payments for Waitlist / Register Interest Bookings — When you Accept a booking, you now have two options: Accept (send an email prompting confirmation and payment) or Accept & Confirm (instantly confirm regardless of payment). When rejecting, choose to notify via email or reject silently.

## Improved

- Improved Search Sorting — Bookings are now consistently ordered by time and name when searching, ensuring a more intuitive and predictable experience.

## Fixed

- Small copy changes — Updated instructions on check-in only roles.

- Space order — Ordering Spaces by the date in which they were created.

- Security on max bookings — Ensuring max guests per booking was always enforced, even with the Waitlist.
