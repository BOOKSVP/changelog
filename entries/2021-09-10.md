## New
 
 

You can now add and remove guest from a booking. In addition you can add the email of the guests attending. This is a useful feature if you are capturing the details of all visitors on the door for either contact tracing or following up post event.

To add or remove guest from a booking simply click on the booking and add and remove guests at the top of the booking module.
