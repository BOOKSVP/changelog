## New
 
 

In order to aid front of house teams using iPads or mobile devices to check in guests we are excited to launch real time live check in.

This means that all devices will update automatically when a guest is checked in without the need to refresh the page.

![CleanShot 2021-09-14 at 14.41.50.gif](https://cloud.headwayapp.co/changelogs_images/images/big/000/075/047-56acb5b257b9e61f53e3d3fa33abe1f56bdbee65.gif)
⬆️ gif showing 1 browser automatically updating without refreshing

In addition to this live alerts will show when bookings are made.

NB. Remember to use partial check in if only part of a group arrive for accurate attendance tracking. To do this click on a booking and just check in the number of guests who have arrived.
