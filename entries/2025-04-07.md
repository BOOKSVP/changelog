## New

- Two Factor Authentication (2FA) — We've implemented 2FA with an Authenticator app across accounts, allowing users to secure their account access and data. To use 2FA, go to your 'Account' Settings and click 'Enable 2FA'. Make sure to record your backup codes to maintain access in case of losing your Authenticator app.

## Improved

- Improved styling on longer booking links displayed in modal windows — they are now truncated visually for a cleaner look.

## Fixed

- Fixed bug that prevented the correct implementation of timezone when filtering, by expanding the number of timezones available to select from.

- Removed all legacy code for event blockers. RIP to one of our favourite first features.
