[new]
 
 

It is now possible to check in part of a booking.

Previously it was only possible to check in the whole booking upon arrival but now you can mark bookings as partially checked in when the whole group do not arrive at once or at all.

- Fully checked in bookings are marked with a green tick. You can check a whole booking in (and out) directly on the bookings view by clicking the tick.
- Partially checked in bookings are marked orange
- Unchecked in bookings are marked grey

How to partially check in some guests on a booking

Partial check in lets you check in just the guests that have actually arrived. To do so simple click on a booking from the bookings view and then check in the number of guests that have arrived.
