An exciting and long awaited feature is finally available!

Don't have time to take names on the door? You can now ask your guests to show their QR code at the door and start checking them in fast and efficiently.

## How do I turn this on for my guests?

Simply head on over to the 'Edit' page for you event and select 'Enable QR Codes' under optional settings and click save.

![CleanShot 2022-03-11 at 15.22.09@2x.png](//cloud.headwayapp.co/changelogs_images/images/big/000/084/369-9b84bfa253d7784d32fea82315f9f6befb3e1fb8.png)

Once this feature is enabled, your guests will see a 'View QR Code' button on their booking confirmation screen.

![CleanShot 2022-03-11 at 15.24.39@2x.png](//cloud.headwayapp.co/changelogs_images/images/big/000/084/371-8edcc115cd6276c2be4ef4e3830dd94ad60fa806.png)

Clicking that button will simply show the code like so

![CleanShot 2022-03-11 at 15.24.46@2x.png](//cloud.headwayapp.co/changelogs_images/images/big/000/084/372-693f4488c24a6daeb5c3033a438c9fbf50a370f9.png)

## How do I scan a QR code?

Scanning a code is easy. You will now see a QR code symbol on your bookings view (for both events and collections). Instead of searching for the name or scrolling through your list, simply tap the QR code icon show below.

![CleanShot 2022-03-11 at 15.09.25@2x.png](//cloud.headwayapp.co/changelogs_images/images/big/000/084/366-d864e534d188640c16b7898461ae627cb52cf6d1.png)

This will bring up the scanner (you may need to accept browser permissions first!) and allow you to scan your guest QR codes all within your bookings view. No need to download an extra app!

![CleanShot 2022-03-11 at 15.15.20@2x.png](//cloud.headwayapp.co/changelogs_images/images/big/000/084/367-b5a3a32f80741a0d5b5979caebfce80828bd8228.png)

A successful scan will automatically show the name, number of guests, booking time and the event they have booked to visit.

You can simply tap 'Check In' and then 'Scan a new code' to move on to your next guest.

![CleanShot 2022-03-11 at 15.17.57@2x.png](//cloud.headwayapp.co/changelogs_images/images/big/000/084/368-79ef83715877ee42d8ff29c3617f7995b16c3835.png)

Suitable error messages will be shown if any guests attempt to check in with incorrect QR codes.

Please note: Only bookings made within the Collection or Event you are using the scanner from will be accepted.

If you have any questions, please contact us using the 'Live Chat' button and we can help you get set up.
