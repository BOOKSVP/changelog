## New
 
Introducing Booking Links 🔗

Previously, we offered 'multi-use' Invites for the purpose of tracking booking sources or as a way of offering exhibitors a link to share with their clients (capped at a maximum number of bookings). However, we found that this caused confusion about the purpose of an 'Invite' and have decided to redefine Invites to be exclusively used for individuals. Invites provide a highly personalised booking experience and can be conveniently sent through the ARTSVP platform so are best suited for specific people.

We have sunset ‘Multi Use Invites’ and replaced them with [Booking Links](https://artsvp.com/blog/custom-booking-links).

![CleanShot 2024-02-08 at 10.51.59@2x.png](https://cloud.headwayapp.co/changelogs_images/images/big/000/122/253-fcebd0c501b0b28e6b54df1f846235dbf2195b99.png)

If you want to quickly create a link to track the booking source for your Event or Collection (e.g. Instagram, Newsletter, etc.), you should use a Booking Link. Simply create a custom name for the link, such as 'VIP', so that your link looks like [fair.artsvp.com/xxx/link/vip](http://fair.artsvp.com/xxx/link/vip).

You can also use Booking Links to allow partners, exhibitors, or anyone else to book your event (without an invite) and control the number of bookings that can be made through the link.
