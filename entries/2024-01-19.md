[new]
 
Feature Updates

Some major improvements that have been made over the last couple months

## Spaces

Events and Collections are now organised into Spaces. You can create new ones or swap the space you are in with the dropdown on the left side of your screen. Feel free to use them as you wish. We recommend creating a new Space for each Art Fair you run.

![CleanShot 2024-01-19 at 11.39.09@2x.png](https://cloud.headwayapp.co/changelogs_images/images/big/000/121/142-2c28a4be06e841975ada2b2680a7a15e46a4d4fb.png)

## Contact Management

Users can now manage their contacts seamlessly through ARTSVP. With contacts; quickly curate lists for invites, tag and add notes for personalisation, and track activity across fairs and events. Request Access.

![photo_5879992313818759672_y.jpg](https://cloud.headwayapp.co/changelogs_images/images/big/000/121/520-71d216decfb6ca5376f678f59e8248995a352ae6.jpg)

## Global Search

Click 'Search' in the navbar or simply use **CMD/CTRL + K ** on your keyboard to bring up global search. This will search everything within the current space you are in.

![CleanShot 2024-01-19 at 11.42.18@2x.png](https://cloud.headwayapp.co/changelogs_images/images/big/000/121/143-9f88e4854efa69546f5d8e8ca974fbf14499d6f7.png)

## All Bookings View

No longer do you have to click on an Event or Collection to view the bookings. Simple go to the All Bookings view to search, filter and download the bookings you need.

![CleanShot 2024-01-19 at 11.44.16@2x.png](https://cloud.headwayapp.co/changelogs_images/images/big/000/121/144-809bf1d3a35acefd0b089d06605542e3440c84fc.png)
