[improved]
 

The bookings view on Events and Collections has now been upgraded to use a date range picker 📅 ➡️ 📅

![CleanShot 2022-09-27 at 11.03.16@2x.png](//cloud.headwayapp.co/changelogs_images/images/big/000/095/833-f2a391862cd719e3c8d41d5f3825ca16dd0e3378.png)

You can now select multiple dates to view at once and be presented with a live check-in count of the dates you have selected.

[fixed]
 

Downloads have now been updated to default to the same date range you have selected in the bookings view. No more blank downloads!
